Tubing

During one of the waterfront sessions my sports group and I went to the lake and my group of four were the first to go. We would get on the tube and then be taken out far into the lake where the driver then tried to make us fall off of the tube by making sharp turns and waves which made it really hard to stay on. After our turn was done we then were dropped off in the middle of the lake where everyone else was. For the remaining time we all hungout in the middle of the lake on surfboards with our sports coaches and our cabin counselors.

IX vs Sports Counselors Dodgeball

So in this game if you are either 15, 16, and 17 years old you will be put in what is called the IX age group which is what I was in this summer, and during one of the days during this week we have a big dodgeball game/match against the sports counselors. This was actually very fun even though we lost 0-3 because before the match we all dress in all black clothing and then we put on body paint and do whatever we want with the designs. After we get ready we take pictures, videos and hangout for a little. Then we get to the dodgeball game and while we are playing we have the younger kids come and watch if they want which is pretty cool and this entire thing was really fun.
